EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Catholic Schools Parramatta Diocese (CSPD) is the second-largest Catholic diocesan school system in NSW, serving 45,646 students across 83 schools in Western Sydney and the Blue Mountains. CSPD completed a major governance transformation in 2023 — new name (formerly CEDP), new CEO Jack de Groot, and a skills-based Board — and launched a Five-Year Strategic Plan in October 2024 and its flagship 'Uplift' teaching framework in August 2025. The system is growing (1.3–2.5% YoY enrolment growth since 2022), financially stretched ($20.6m operating deficit in 2023 with $19m/year salary escalation locked in), and investing heavily in infrastructure, technology, and workforce quality.
