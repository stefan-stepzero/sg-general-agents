## CSPD-Specific Persuasion Context

### Identity frame
CSPD is a mission-driven Catholic organisation. Frame everything through their stated pillars: Mission, Relevance, Sustainability, People. Proposals that align with mission will land differently than pure efficiency arguments.

### Key anchors to use
- BCE Copilot: 9.3 hours/week saved per teacher (strongest quantified peer outcome)
- CECG Catalyst: 75% reading proficiency vs 66% national average (Uplift's direct ancestor)
- CSPD's own $20.6M operating deficit (loss frame for efficiency proposals)
- 500+ families turned away at Westmead (scarcity/demand frame)
- 53.4% LBOTE vs 36% sector average (differentiation opportunity)

### Who we're pitching to
The likely initial conversation is with Patrick Ellis (EGM Learning Outcomes) or Frances Waterford (EGM IT). Both are relatively new in post and need early wins. Frame proposals as helping them deliver on their mandate, not adding to their workload.

### What NOT to do
- Don't criticise their "domed" AI policy — position around it

- Don't lead with technology — lead with student outcomes and teacher experience
- Don't propose a platform replacement — they just deployed Canvas system-wide
